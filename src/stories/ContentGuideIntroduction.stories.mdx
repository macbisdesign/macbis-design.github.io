<h2>Purpose</h2>


This is the house content style guide for OneMAC. It will guide  creators in writing clear, consistent content throughout the product ecosystem. This guide is the source of truth for content. 

<h2>Scope</h2>
<p>This guide applies to any written content within the OneMAC product. It covers content structure, best practices for component content, and content-specific accessibility considerations.</p>

<h2>How to use</h2>
<p>

Refer to this guide whenever creating content. Here are a few ways you might use this guide:

<ul>
<li>Before drafting new content: review voice and tone and grammar and style guidelines.</li>
<li>While creating content: refer to guidance on content structure, accessibility, and word choice.</li>
<li>When itâ€™s almost ready for review: look over grammar and punctuation guidance.</li>
</ul>
